Constant Reader Guide is a small site for people making their way through Stephen King's body of work — where to start, what order to read a series in, and which books are worth your time.

The name comes from King himself, who signs off his book introductions "Constant Reader" — his term for the readers who stick with him book after book.

This site is independently run and is not affiliated with Stephen King, his publishers, or any studio producing adaptations of his work.
